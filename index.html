Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

Anchor: Breathwork ("Anchor," "we," "our") is a breathwork and meditation app for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Anchor accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Collect

None. Anchor does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

All app data — including your breathing session history, preferences, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up via iCloud (managed by Apple, not by us).

Apple HealthKit

If you grant permission, Anchor writes your completed breathing sessions to Apple Health as Mindful Minutes and reads your existing mindful session data. This data is handled entirely by Apple's HealthKit framework and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Anchor never accesses HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or data mining purposes, in accordance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

Third-Party Services

Anchor does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Anchor does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains no user accounts, social features, or data collection mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
